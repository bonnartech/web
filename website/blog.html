blog post

Yet another major release by rapper Nivini.Titled rythm and booze,this project brings to light the young artist's passion for his homecountry as he portrays a flourishing post independence Kenya.Accompanying the slides of film is a nostalgic feel with a laidback instrumental and calmly written lyrics. A huge advocate of the bare maximum(doing the most with the least you have)he takes it to the edge by recording and engineering the sound on a friends laptop using cheap equipment he sourced online and going on to edit raw stock free footage he stumbled upon on youtube on his old iphone 7.With no background in media or film this was purely a case of trial and error.Did it work out? You be the judge.Watch Rythm and booze(the short film) by clicking the link below.
